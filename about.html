About Us

This is the place to go for your English football needs

We are located in the IFSC in Dublin, Drop in anytime!

We are a group of football fans dedicated to providing news on the latest games from the Premier League, the Championship and the European competitions, our focus for Europe is also on the English sides seeing how they fair outside of their home comforts.

Robbie is here to give his knowledge on the Premier League, a religious follower of the top division, his favourite team being Man United and he is here to tell you the ins and outs of the league

Daire knows all about the championship and he likes to go in depth on individual games, Daire is our betting man and the one to follow if you need a tip, he will tell you who is in form in the league and who to avoid like the plague.

Dale is the man for European football, he follows the English sides to see how they fair in Europes top competitions, as well as this he has a keen following of the premier league and the other top divisions in Europe to see who will be in the competitions next year.
