European competitions

champions league and europa league

Posted by Dale Byrne on December 3, 2015

The Champions League

Currently in the champions league there are four premier league teams left trying to qualify for the next stage.

Manchester City are the one premier league side that have already qualified and are trying to achieve top spot in the group.

Chelsea, Manchester United and Arsenal are in first, second and third in their respective groups and have to hope results go their way on the final round of games to see if they qualify for the next round.

Slide Show

The Europa League

In the other European competition, the Europa league there are currently two English teams and one Scottish team in the competition.

The two English teams Liverpool and Tottenham both sit atop their respective groups and have qualified for the next round whilst trying to finish the group at the top, Celtic however are bottom of their group and can't go any further in the competition.

What's next

Champions League

I believe all of the teams mentioned have a chance to go far in the competition, Chelsea and Manchester City have the best chances of going furthest, Man City look the strongest but for Chelsea they have no other chances than this competition so they have to go far.

Europa League

For this competition I believe both of the English teams have a real chance of winning the tournament, they are both playing great football and look to be challenging for trophies this season as well as champions league football next season.

Next Season

For next season the same names are always thrown about (the top 6) for the champions league spots and europa league spots but chelsea are going to really have to step up their game if they don't want to fall behind, all of the other top teams look likely candidates to challenge for the title and Leicester look likely for European football next season.
